Candidate

This is a person that is running to be nominated as a general election candidate for President of the United States.

Joe Biden

Joe Biden is an American politician who served as the 47th vice president of the United States from 2009 to 2017 alongside President Barack Obama. Joe Biden is the 2020 Democratic Presidential nominee, running against current President Donald Trump. Joe Biden is running alongside Senator Kamala Harris.

Polling Place

This is a location that voters can visit to cast a ballot for the candidates they prefer.

Herbert C Young Community Center

The Herbert C Young Community Center is located at 101 Wilkinson Ave, Cary, North Carolina. One-stop early voting for the November 2020 general runs Oct 15 - 31, 2020.

Sample Ballot

This is a document that potential voters can use to practice voting and to preview which candidates are up for election.

Wake County, North Carolina Sample Ballot

Depicted on the left is a Sample Ballot for residents of Wake County, North Carolina. Your ballot will defer based on where you reside. In addition to the Election of Federal Offices, State Elections are also being held at the same time. The ballot lists the many different positions which elections are being held for, such as the State Attorney General, The State Governor, The State Supreme Court Chief Justice Seat, and many more. On page two of this sample ballot, you can vote for the District Office, County Office, and Nonpartisan Office representatives.
